Vinandi — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 12, 2025

Welcome to Vinandi — a modern educational platform that helps users learn through structured video lessons. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use the Vinandi mobile application or website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a personalized and secure learning experience.

a. Information you provide:

  • Account details: Name, email address, and password (securely encrypted).
  • Payment information: Processed securely via trusted third-party payment providers (such as Stripe, Razorpay, or PayPal). Vinandi does not store full card details.
  • Support messages: Information you send to us via email or feedback forms.

b. Information collected automatically:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, videos watched, and app interactions to improve user experience.
  • Device data: Device model, operating system version, app version, and identifiers.
  • Cookies / local storage: Used to maintain login sessions and preferences.

2. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To deliver personalized educational video content.
  • To process secure payments for subscriptions or courses.
  • To improve app features, usability, and security.
  • To send important notifications, updates, or respond to support inquiries.

We do not sell or rent your personal data to anyone.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share limited information only with:

  • Service providers: For hosting, analytics, and payment processing.
  • Legal authorities: If required by law or to prevent fraud or misuse.
  • Business transfers: If Vinandi undergoes a merger or acquisition, data may be transferred as part of that process.

4. Data Retention and Security

  • We retain your information only as long as necessary for providing our services and legal compliance.
  • All passwords are securely encrypted.
  • Payment information is handled exclusively by certified payment processors.
  • We regularly review security protocols to protect user data from unauthorized access.

5. Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal information.
  • Request correction or deletion of your account data.
  • Withdraw consent for data use where applicable.

You can contact us anytime to manage your data or delete your account.

6. Children’s Privacy

Vinandi is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such data, we delete it immediately.

7. Third-Party Services

Vinandi may use third-party services for analytics, hosting, or payment. These providers follow their own privacy and security practices in compliance with applicable laws.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted here with a new effective date. Continued use of Vinandi means you accept the updated policy.
